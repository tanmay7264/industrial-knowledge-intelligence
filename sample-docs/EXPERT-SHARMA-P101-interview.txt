INTERVIEW NOTES — TACIT KNOWLEDGE

On pump P-101 vibration diagnostics:
"If vibration and bearing temperature increase together on P-101, check shaft alignment
before replacing bearings. In three of the last four vibration events we traced to
misalignment, not bearing wear. The alignment check was skipped in the 2023 bearing
replacement — we paid for that with a repeat failure within 90 days."

On mechanical seal failures:
"Seal failures on P-101 usually follow misalignment or dry-running during startup.
Never run the pump dry for more than 30 seconds during priming."

On organizational memory:

bearing diagnostics, but the real fix for recurring P-101 vibration is Alignment
Procedure Rev B — not just bearing swap."
