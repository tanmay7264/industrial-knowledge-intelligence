Section: Troubleshooting — Cavitation and Bearing Failures

Cavitation Symptoms:
- Intermittent rumbling or crackling noise at impeller
- Fluctuating discharge pressure and flow
- Erosion marks on impeller vanes
- Accelerated bearing wear due to hydraulic imbalance

Root Causes of Recurring Bearing Failure:
1. Insufficient NPSH available at suction
2. Shaft misalignment exceeding 0.05 mm
3. Pipe strain on pump casing
4. Operating beyond BEP (best efficiency point)

Alignment Requirements:
- Laser alignment mandatory after ANY bearing replacement
- Maximum allowable misalignment: 0.05 mm radial, 0.03 mm angular
- Re-check alignment after 48 hours of operation (thermal growth)

Recommended Corrective Actions:
- Verify suction conditions and strainer cleanliness
- Perform laser shaft alignment
- Ensure operation within 70–110% of BEP flow
- Document alignment results in CMMS before closing work order
