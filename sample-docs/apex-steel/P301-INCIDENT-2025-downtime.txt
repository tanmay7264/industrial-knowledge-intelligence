INCIDENT REPORT — PRODUCTION DOWNTIME
Asset: P-301 Cooling Water Pump
Date: 2025-08-14
Incident ID: INC-2025-P301-001
Severity: Major (4 hours downtime)

Summary:
Cooling water pump P-301 failed during peak production. Rolling mill cooling interrupted for 4 hours. Production loss estimated at ₹12 lakh.

Timeline:
- 14:20 — High vibration alarm, pump tripped on bearing temperature
- 14:35 — Emergency bearing replacement initiated
- 18:20 — Pump returned to service after bearing swap

Root Cause (preliminary):
- Bearing failure due to misalignment (not confirmed — no alignment check done)
- Cavitation suspected based on operator logs from prior week

Lessons Learned:
- Bearing replacement without alignment verification leads to repeat failure
- Cooling water pump fleet (P-301, P-305, P-318) needs group reliability review
- NPSH verification should be part of every pump inspection

Corrective Actions (open):
- [ ] Complete formal RCA
- [ ] Implement mandatory alignment check after bearing work
- [ ] Review suction system design for all cooling water pumps

Related equipment: P-305, P-318, HX-105
