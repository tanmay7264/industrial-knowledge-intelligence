INTERNAL AUDIT FINDING — AUDIT-2024-MAINT-014
Date: 2024-11-05
Auditor: Quality Assurance — Maintenance Practices
Scope: Rotating equipment maintenance — alignment verification

FINDING
Work orders for bearing replacement on critical pumps (including P-101) were closed
without documented alignment verification in 4 of 12 cases reviewed.

ROOT CAUSE OF AUDIT FINDING
Maintenance checklist did not mandate alignment check post bearing replacement.
Alignment Procedure Rev B existed but was not referenced in work order templates.

RECOMMENDATION
1. Update CMMS work order template for bearing replacements to include alignment sign-off
2. Link SOP-MAINT-P101 Rev B changelog to all P-101 maintenance work orders
3. Retrain maintenance technicians on Alignment Procedure Rev B

LESSONS LEARNED
Alignment check skipped in previous failure (INC-2023-P101) directly contributed to
repeat vibration event (INC-2024-P101). Organizational memory must capture this pattern.

STATUS: Open — corrective action due 2025-03-31
