Title: Fixed Water Spray (Deluge) System — LPG Storage Area
Regulatory Reference: OISD-STD-116, OISD-STD-117

1. SCOPE
This specification covers the fixed water spray (deluge) system protecting the
LPG storage bullets B-301 and B-302 and the associated pump house.

2. DESIGN BASIS
2.1 Water application rate: 10.2 litres per minute per square metre of vessel
    surface area, in accordance with OISD-STD-116.
2.2 Fire water storage: sized for the single largest fire risk for an aggregate
    duration of 4 hours.
2.3 Fire water pumps: two pumps provided — one electric motor driven and one
    diesel engine driven — each rated for 100% of design demand (410 m3/hr at
    7 barg).

3. DETECTION AND ACTUATION
Heat detectors and manual call points initiate the deluge valve. Audible and
visual alarms sound in the control room and field per OISD-GDN-206.

4. EQUIPMENT
Deluge valve DV-301. Fire water pump FWP-01 (electric), FWP-02 (diesel).
Jockey pump JP-01 maintains ring main pressure.

5. INSPECTION AND TESTING
Monthly visual inspection of nozzles. Annual full flow test. Portable
extinguishers in the area inspected monthly per OISD-STD-142.
