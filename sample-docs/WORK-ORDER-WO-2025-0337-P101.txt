Equipment Tag: P-101 (Hydraulic Feed Pump, Crude Distillation Unit)
Related Vessel: V-201 (Suction Drum)

1. WORK DESCRIPTION
Replace mechanical seal on pump P-101 following high seal-flush temperature trend
observed during quarterly vibration survey at CML-P101-3. Inspect coupling and
re-align to OEM tolerance.

2. APPLICABLE PROCEDURES & STANDARDS
- SOP-MAINT-014 (Preventive Maintenance — Hydraulic Pump P-101).
- Mechanical integrity inspection per OISD-STD-128, clause 6.3 (in-service
  inspection of rotating equipment auxiliaries).
- Pressure relief valve PRV-101A to be left undisturbed; recalibration tracked
  separately under OISD-STD-106, clause 5.2 (test interval not exceeding 36 months).

3. ISOLATION & SAFETY
Lockout/tagout applied. Work authorised under Permit to Work per OISD-STD-105.
Confirm pump isolated from V-201 before seal removal. PPE mandatory.

4. PARTS
Mechanical seal kit MSK-P101 (OEM). Gasket set GS-14. Seal flush filter.

5. ASSIGNED
Fitter: D. Souza. Supervisor: R. Mehta. QA hold point: S. Iyer to witness
re-alignment readings before box-up.

6. CLOSEOUT
Record final alignment, seal flush pressure (target 1.5 barg above suction),
and update the equipment history in the CMMS per ISO 9001:2015 clause 7.5.
