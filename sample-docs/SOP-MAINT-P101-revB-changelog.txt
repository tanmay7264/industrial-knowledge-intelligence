Revision B — Effective 2024-10-01
Author: Engineer Sharma
Approver: Maintenance Manager

REVISION B CHANGES (from Rev A)

NEW: Alignment Procedure Rev B
- Mandatory laser alignment verification after ANY bearing replacement on P-101
- Maximum allowable offset: 0.05 mm (per Grundfos manual Section 6)
- Alignment re-check required 30 days after foundation work within 5 m of P-101 base

UPDATED: Mechanical seal inspection interval
- Unchanged at 2000 operating hours
- Added note: seal failures often follow misalignment — check alignment first

UPDATED: Corrective action for high vibration
- Step 1: Check alignment before ordering bearings
- Step 2: Reference vendor manual Section 6 diagnostic sequence
- Step 3: Escalate to Engineer Sharma if vibration > 7.1 mm/s RMS

RATIONALE
Lessons from INC-2023-P101 and INC-2024-P101 — bearing replacement without alignment
caused repeat failures. Audit finding AUDIT-2024-MAINT-014.
