INCIDENT REPORT — INC-2024-P101
Date: 2024-09-14
Asset: Hydraulic Pump P-101
Reported by: Shift Operator Mehta
Investigator: Engineer Sharma

SYMPTOMS
- Vibration increased from 2.1 to 6.8 mm/s RMS over 48 hours
- Drive-end bearing temperature rose from 58°C to 74°C
- No audible cavitation; flow rate stable at 180 m³/h

FAILURE MODE
High vibration with concurrent bearing temperature rise

ROOT CAUSE
Shaft misalignment between motor and pump coupling. Laser alignment check revealed
0.18 mm offset — exceeds vendor limit of 0.05 mm. Previous work order WO-2024-0891
addressed alignment but foundation settling caused re-misalignment within 6 months.

RESOLUTION
- Pump stopped and locked out per SOP-MAINT-P101
- Laser realignment performed per Alignment Procedure Rev B
- Coupling inspected — no damage
- Vibration post-repair: 2.3 mm/s RMS

OUTCOME
Resolved — pump returned to service 2024-09-16

LESSONS LEARNED
- Alignment check must be repeated 30 days after any foundation work near P-101
- Vibration + temperature together → check alignment before replacing bearings
  (per Engineer Sharma field guidance)
