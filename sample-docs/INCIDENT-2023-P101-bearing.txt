INCIDENT REPORT — INC-2023-P101
Date: 2023-06-22
Asset: Hydraulic Pump P-101
Reported by: Maintenance Technician Khan
Investigator: Engineer Sharma

SYMPTOMS
- Abnormal noise from drive-end bearing housing
- Vibration 5.2 mm/s RMS
- Bearing temperature 68°C

FAILURE MODE
Premature bearing failure (drive-end)

ROOT CAUSE
Bearings replaced during scheduled maintenance without post-replacement alignment check.
Misalignment of 0.12 mm caused accelerated bearing wear within 90 days of installation.

RESOLUTION
- Bearing replacement (SKF 6312-C3)
- Alignment check SKIPPED — noted as deviation in work order
- Repeat failure occurred 2024-09 (see INC-2024-P101)

OUTCOME
Resolved at time of report — but recurrence within 15 months

LESSONS LEARNED
- Never close a bearing replacement work order without alignment verification
- Alignment Procedure Rev B now mandatory for all P-101 bearing work
- Audit finding AUDIT-2024-MAINT-014 raised against this gap
