STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Document No: SOP-PROC-009   Rev: 1   Effective Date: 2014-08-01
Title: Process Hazard Analysis (PHA) Programme
Owner: Process Safety Section
Status: ACTIVE  (Note: scheduled review date 2019-08 — review NOT completed)

1. PURPOSE
This procedure governs how Process Hazard Analysis studies (HAZOP) are planned,
conducted and revalidated for the refinery process units.

2. WHEN A PHA IS REQUIRED
2.1 For all new process facilities prior to commissioning.
2.2 For significant modifications, via the Management of Change process.

3. REVALIDATION INTERVAL
3.1 Existing facilities shall have their HAZOP studies revalidated at intervals
    not exceeding TEN (10) YEARS.
3.2 Basis: OISD-GDN-178 (2002 edition), Section 4.

4. STUDY TEAM
Led by a qualified facilitator with operations, maintenance and process
engineering representation.

5. CLOSE-OUT
Recommendations are logged and reviewed periodically by the Process Safety
Section.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
DOCUMENT CONTROL NOTE: This procedure has not been updated since Rev 1 (2014).
The referenced 2002 edition of OISD-GDN-178 has since been revised; the current
revalidation expectation for existing facilities is a maximum of FIVE (5) YEARS.
This SOP still mandates a 10-year interval and is overdue for revision.
