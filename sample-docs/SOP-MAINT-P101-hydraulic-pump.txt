STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
Document No: SOP-MAINT-014  Rev: 3  Effective Date: 2025-02-15
Title: Preventive Maintenance — Hydraulic Pump P-101
Owner: Rotating Equipment Section, Maintenance Department
Quality System Reference: ISO 9001:2015 Clause 7.1.5 (Monitoring and Measuring Resources)

1. PURPOSE
This procedure defines the preventive maintenance requirements for hydraulic pump
P-101, the primary feed pump on the Crude Distillation Unit. It ensures reliable
operation and compliance with the plant mechanical integrity program.

2. SCOPE
Applies to centrifugal hydraulic pump P-101 and its associated pressure relief
valve PRV-101A. Related equipment: pressure vessel V-201 (suction drum).

3. MAINTENANCE INTERVALS
3.1 Lubrication of bearings: every 720 operating hours (monthly).
3.2 Vibration analysis: quarterly, with readings logged at condition monitoring
    location CML-P101-3.
3.3 Mechanical seal inspection: every 2000 operating hours.
3.4 Full overhaul and internal inspection: every 8000 operating hours, or
    24 months, whichever comes first.
3.5 Pressure relief valve PRV-101A recalibration: every 36 months, in line with
    OISD-STD-106. Set pressure shall not exceed the maximum allowable working
    pressure of 18 barg.

4. PARAMETERS
Normal discharge pressure: 14.5 barg. High-pressure alarm: 16 barg. Trip: 17 barg.
Normal bearing temperature: 65 C. High alarm: 85 C.

5. RESPONSIBLE PERSONNEL
Maintenance Engineer: R. Mehta. Reliability Inspector: S. Iyer.
All work is authorised under the plant Permit to Work system per OISD-STD-105.

6. RECORDS
Completed work orders are retained in the CMMS for the life of the equipment in
accordance with ISO 9001:2015.
