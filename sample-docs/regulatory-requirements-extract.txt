PLANT COMPLIANCE REFERENCE — REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS EXTRACT
Document No: COMP-REF-001  Rev: 1  Date: 2025-01-10
Purpose: Consolidated extract of key statutory and standard requirements
applicable to the refinery, with the controlling clauses.

FIRE PROTECTION (OISD-STD-116)
- Fixed water spray systems for pressure storage vessels shall be designed for a
  minimum application rate of 10.2 L/min/m2 of vessel surface area.
- Fire water shall be available for the single largest fire risk for at least
  4 hours, served by one electric and one diesel driven pump.

MECHANICAL INTEGRITY (OISD-STD-128, API 510)
- Pressure vessels shall undergo annual external visual inspection and internal
  inspection on a remaining-life schedule, with records retained for vessel life.
- Pressure relief valves shall be tested and recalibrated at intervals not
  exceeding 36 months (OISD-STD-106).

PROCESS SAFETY (OISD-STD-105)
- A Permit to Work system shall control all hot work, confined space entry and
  excavation. Hot work in classified areas requires gas testing below 1% LEL with
  continuous monitoring.

EMERGENCY PREPAREDNESS (OISD-GDN-206)
- An on-site emergency plan shall be documented and tested by mock drills at least
  once per quarter.
- Audible/visual alarm and public address systems shall alert all personnel and
  be tested periodically.

OCCUPATIONAL HEALTH & SAFETY (Factory Act 1948)
- Personal protective equipment shall be provided and its use enforced in
  operating areas.
- Workers in hazardous processes shall undergo periodic medical examination with
  records maintained.

QUALITY MANAGEMENT (ISO 9001:2015)
- Documented procedures and retained records shall demonstrate control of
  inspection, maintenance and calibration activities.
